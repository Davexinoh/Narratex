# Narratex

Discover market narratives before they trend.

Narratex is an AI-powered narrative intelligence engine that detects emerging crypto narratives by analyzing market signals and predicting which sectors and tokens are gaining momentum.

---

## Problem

Crypto markets move in narratives.

Examples include:

- AI infrastructure
- DePIN compute
- Gaming ecosystems
- Layer 2 scaling

Most traders detect these narratives **after they trend**.

---

## Solution

Narratex monitors signals across the crypto ecosystem and detects narrative momentum before it becomes mainstream.

It analyzes:

- market signals
- token activity
- narrative clustering
- momentum scoring

Then predicts emerging narratives and the tokens most likely to benefit.
