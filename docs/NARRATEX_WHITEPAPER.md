# Narratex Whitepaper

## Overview

Narratex is a narrative intelligence engine designed to detect emerging crypto narratives before they become mainstream.

Crypto markets are heavily driven by narratives such as:

- AI infrastructure
- DePIN compute
- gaming ecosystems
- Layer 2 scaling

Narratex analyzes market signals and clusters them into narratives, then calculates narrative momentum to predict which sectors are gaining traction.

---

## System Architecture

Narratex operates as a modular pipeline.

Market Signals  
↓  
Signal Collector  
↓  
Narrative Extractor  
↓  
Momentum Engine  
↓  
Prediction API  
↓  
Dashboard

---

## Signal Collector

The signal collector retrieves market data from external APIs such as exchanges.

Collected signals include:

- price change percentage
- trading volume
- token activity

These signals form the raw dataset for narrative analysis.

---

## Narrative Extraction

The narrative extractor clusters signals into thematic narratives using keyword detection.

Examples:

AI Infrastructure  
DePIN Compute  
Gaming Infrastructure  

Each signal contributes to a narrative score.

---

## Momentum Engine

The momentum engine calculates narrative strength.

Confidence is computed based on signal frequency.

Example formula:

confidence = min(score × 20, 100)

Narratives are classified into:

- Weak Signal
- Emerging Narrative
- Strong Narrative

---

## Token Association

Each narrative is associated with tokens that historically benefit from that sector.

Example:

AI Infrastructure  
FET  
TAO  
RNDR  

This allows Narratex to identify which assets may benefit from narrative growth.

---

## Visualization Layer

Narratex provides a dashboard with multiple visual tools:

Narrative Cards  
Narrative Timeline  
Narrative Heatmap  
Trending Token Radar

These visualizations allow users to quickly identify emerging trends.

## Future Improvements

Future versions of Narratex may include:

## Conclusion

Narratex transforms raw market signals into narrative intelligence.

By detecting narrative momentum early, the system helps users understand how narratives shape crypto markets before they fully emerge.
